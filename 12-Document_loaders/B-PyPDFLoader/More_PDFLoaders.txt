there are different type of pdf loader for different use cases.

PyPDFLoader --> for simple plain text based pdfs

PDFPlumberLoader -->  for pdfs with complex layouts, tables, and images

UnstructuredPDFLoader or AmazonTextractPDFLoader --> for scanned pdfs or image based pdfs

PyMuPDFLoader --> for pdfs with layout and image data

UnstructuredPDFLoader --> use this for best structure extraction
